About

A little about me

I came to photography the way most people do — slowly, then all at once. I started with a Canon A-1 and a roll of film, and it turned into something I couldn't put down. Now I shoot across multiple systems — 35mm film, DSLR, and mirrorless — depending on what the moment calls for.

I'm based in Ocala and spend a lot of time out in Florida's parks and natural spaces. Wildlife photography pulled me in early — there's something about waiting for the right moment with a bird or an animal that teaches you a lot about patience and light. I'm still learning, still growing, and this portfolio is where I track that progress.
